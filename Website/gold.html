What's it all about?

Gold is a form of an in-game currency that you can use to upgrade your stats.

How much gold do I have?

Your gold is a part of the status report generated by the following command:

@Justine#1053 stats

You can also use a direct command to get the gold amount. (in case you're too insecure about that Intelligence stat)

@Justine#1053 gold

How much gold does LeMorrow have?

Asking the real questions, are we?

To check someone else's gold status, you can overload the gold command:

@Justine#1053 gold @LeMorrow6627

Here's a fun challenge, use it on someone who hasn't given collection consent or a BOT. =)

How do I get more gold?

You can get gold in one of two ways.

Gold digging

You can spend anywhere from 1 to 8 hours digging gold. After that time passes, you'll see how much gold you found. EZ

To start looking for gold, use the gold dig command with the amount of hours you want to look for.

As an example, the following command will make you search for 5 hours:

@Justine#1053 gold dig 5

I hate my job

Tired of working? You can cancel your digging job.

@Justine#1053 gold dig cancel

I managed to dig for 5 hours, now what?

After your digging is done, you'll be prompted to collect your reward with the following command:

@Justine#1053 gold dig reward

HOWEVER! If you're too slow, someone else can steal your reward with:

@Justine#1053 steal reward @You#001

But don't worry, there is a 70% chance they will fail. If that happens, your gold gets automatically added to your account. =)

Missions

You can also go on a mission to get some risky reward. More on that in the mission docs.
